How To Laser Cut

  1. Develop your design in a CAD program

    Using a Computer-Aided Design software such as Inkscape or Solidworks, create the design you want to laser cut.

  2. Convert your design into a DXF file

    DXF files are understood by laser cutters. This can often be done through save or export functions.

  3. Bring your DXF file to a laser cutter and begin cutting

    Make sure to grab a buddy and keep an eye on your cut as it proceeds. For formal training and laser cutter access, please consult the Ideate website here: CMU Ideate
